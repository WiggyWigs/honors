Event Recap
Season Event #19 — U.S. Open
Greg Wigton won the U.S. Open with 302.8 — his 7th win of the season and first major of the year. Worth noting: he entered the final round in 4th place and somehow walked out on top, apparently without checking his roster. Sullivan (273.4) and Flores (270.4) were close but couldn't close it. Kevin McNamara finished 4th, Bresnahan last. Kevin's winless streak now sits at 16 events — a personal record and the worst active streak in the league.
Season Summary
2026 Championship Race
Wigton leads Flores by 41.3 with 10 events left — the second-tightest gap at this stage in league history. The only time a leader was closer and lost? 2023, when Bresnahan held a 23.7-point edge and Wigton reeled him in. Six cut events remain, so there's still real ground to be made up. Sullivan at 146.6 back is a long shot. Everyone else is playing for pride.
Next Up
Travelers Championship
Back to a regular Tour Event this week at TPC River Highlands. Wigton carries a 41.3-point lead that's thin enough to evaporate in a single bad week — Flores knows it. Ten events left, and the window for anyone outside the top two to matter is closing fast.
